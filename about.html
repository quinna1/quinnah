CS student, public transit nerd


Hi, I'm Quinna. I was born in Boston, grew up in Jakarta, and relocated some 10,000 miles to the suburbs of Detroit. This summer, I'll be spending six weeks in Chengdu, China for a study abroad program as I continue my search for new experiences outside of my comfort zone.

As a kid, I used to spend hours poring over maps and travel guides (a quirk that led me to runner-up in the middle school geography bee without any formal preparation) and even concocted my own cities, loosely based off ones I knew only through travel guides. Now as I move to the adult world, I aspire to combine my love for tech and cities through the emerging field of urban science, the study of applying big data to big problems in big cities.

Outside of that, you can find me writing short stories, running in the rain, and trying exotic flavors of everything (matcha and taro are my obsessions). I'm also passionate about Asian-American advocacy and immigration rights.
